ABOUT

We are a family owned restaurant that the locals love. We have been around for thirty-five years, and we plan to stay for at least thirty-five more. Tourists love our location on the Grand Canal because they can sit and look out onto the water. The view during a sunset is incredible and hard to miss out on.

Our chef, Adrian Hassan, is a dedicated father of two, husband, and worker. He loves to get creative with his new dishes and his plating. He has studied the culinary arts all over the world, but he specialized in Italian cuisine. He loves to go out and communicate with the customers. He also loves to get feedback from all the tourists and locals. He is always striving to get better and to make the customers even happier every time they visit us.
